Baker pipelines can fetch, transform and store records in a flash thanks to a high-performant and fully-parallel implementation.

Baker is open-source and written in Go by NextRoll, inc..

{{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="primary" >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-link" title="Modular pipeline" %}} Baker uses modular components in a 4-levels stack: inputs, filters, outputs and uploads. Each component of a level can connect with any other from the previous and the next one. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-shipping-fast" title="High performances" %}} Baker is fully parallel and maximizes usage of both CPU-bound and I/O bound pipelines.
